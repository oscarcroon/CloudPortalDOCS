# Certificates

The certificates module manages the lifecycle of your SSL/TLS certificates — from ordering to automatic renewal.

## What you can do

- **Order certificates** — order new certificates for your domains.
- **Deployment via agents** — distribute certificates to your servers.
- **Discovery & inventory** — find and keep track of existing certificates.
