# Customer assignment

Customer assignment is about connecting customers to the right partner. Depending on your role, you create either providers (as a distributor) or customer organizations (as a provider). This page shows how to do both.

## Create a provider (as a distributor)

As a distributor you assign customers by creating providers beneath you.

1. Go to **Providers** in the partner section.
2. Click **Create provider**.
3. Enter the provider's **name** and **company registration number**. The registration number is checked automatically against the company register.
4. Enter the email address of the provider's **owner account**. This is the person who becomes the main contact and is allowed to administer the provider.
5. Save. The provider is created and you are taken to its detail view.

::note
When you enter the owner's email, the portal checks whether an account with that address already exists. If the account already exists it is linked; otherwise a new account is created and an invitation is sent.
::

::tip
A provider can be linked to one or more distributors. When you create the provider you choose which distributors it should belong to.
::

## Create a customer organization (as a provider)

As a provider you assign customers by creating organizations.

1. Go to **Organizations** in the partner section and click **Create organization**. You can also start from tenant management via **Create organization**.
2. **Step 1 – Organization details:** choose which provider the organization should belong to (preselected if you start from a provider), enter a **name** and optionally a company registration number. The registration number is checked against the company register.
3. **Step 2 – Owner account:** enter the email address of the person who will be the organization's contact and owner.
4. Save. The organization is created under the selected provider.

::warning
Verify the company registration number before you save. If the register lookup shows that the number is incorrect, registration is blocked until it is resolved.
::

## Link an owner account

When you enter the owner's email address, the portal helps you assign the right person:

- **Existing account** — the address already belongs to a user. The account is linked to the new provider or organization.
- **New account** — the address is unknown. A new account is created and the person is invited.

This way the customer is always linked to a responsible person right from creation.

## Move and deactivate customers

- A provider's status can be set to **archived** to temporarily take it out of use, and **restored** when needed. See [Tenants and organizations](/en/partner/tenants-and-organizations).
- Organizations can be active or inactive. An inactive organization remains but is not in use.

::note
Creating a provider or organization provides structure and access. What the partner's team is actually allowed to do at the customer is controlled separately via roles and permissions.
::
