# Partner

The partner section is for MSP partners who manage several customer organizations in CloudPortal.

## What you can do

- **Tenants & organizations** — administer the customers you're responsible for.
- **Customer assignment** — link customers to you as a partner.
- **Roles and permissions** — control what your team can do at the customers.
- **Support flows** — handle support cases for your customers.

## Configure a customer's organization

You set up SSO, SCIM, custom domain and branding per customer under **Partner → Tenants → [customer]**. The steps are the same as for an organization:

- [Single sign-on (SSO)](/en/organization/sso)
- [SCIM provisioning](/en/organization/scim)
- [Custom domain](/en/organization/custom-domain)
- [Branding](/en/organization/branding)
