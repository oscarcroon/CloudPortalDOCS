# Autodiscover

Autodiscover helps you find DNS zones that belong to your organization and activate them in the portal, so you can start managing their records. It is a convenient way to get started if you have several domains.

## Run autodiscover

1. Go to **Domains & DNS** and open **Autodiscover** (you can also reach it via DNS zones).
2. The search starts automatically when you open the page. You can also click **Run Autodiscover** to search again.
3. When the search is complete, the zones that were found are listed. Each zone shows the domain name and which server it lives on.

## Activate zones

For each zone found you see a status:

- **Activated** — the zone already belongs to your organization and is managed in the portal.
- **Available** — the zone can be activated.

To activate zones:

1. Select the zones you want to activate with the checkboxes (available zones are pre-selected).
2. Click **Activate selected** to activate the selected ones, or **Activate all** to activate all available zones.
3. The list refreshes and the activated zones now show as **Activated**.

Once a zone is activated, it appears under [DNS zones](/en/domains-and-dns/dns-zones) where you can open it and manage its records.

## If no zones are found

If the search does not find any zones, a message about it is shown. This usually means that your domain is not yet prepared for automatic discovery.

::note
For a domain to be found automatically, it needs to be configured so that it can be linked to your organization. If autodiscover does not find your domains, contact your administrator and they will help you prepare or activate them.
::
