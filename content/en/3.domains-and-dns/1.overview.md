# Domains & DNS

In the domains and DNS module you manage your domains and their DNS records.

## What you can do

- **DNS zones** — view and manage your zones.
- **DNS records** — create and edit records (A, AAAA, CNAME, MX, TXT and more).
- **Cloudflare DNS** — manage zones hosted at Cloudflare.
- **Redirects** — set up redirects.
- **Autodiscover** — configure autodiscover for email clients.
