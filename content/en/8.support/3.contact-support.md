# Contact support

If you can't find the answer in the documentation or under [Errors and troubleshooting](/en/support/errors-and-troubleshooting), we're happy to help.

## How to reach us

1. Open **Support** in the portal (the link is in the top menu and under help/settings).
2. On the support page you'll find our current contact channels – email and phone.
3. Contact support via the email address and phone number shown there.

::note
Contact details are always shown in the portal on the support page. Use the details there, since they may vary depending on your provider or distributor.
::

::tip
Shortcut: the top menu has shortcuts to **Documentation**, **Support**, and **Status page**. The status page corresponds to [Service status](/en/support/service-status).
::

## Turn to your administrator first in these cases

Some matters are resolved fastest by your own organization or provider administrator rather than support:

- **You need more permissions** (you lack access to a module or feature).
- **You need to be added or removed** as a user.
- **Two-factor authentication needs to be reset** because you've lost both the app and the backup codes.
- **SSO sign-in** for your organization – contact whoever administers your identity provider.

## What to have ready

The more information you provide, the faster we can help you. Please include:

1. **What you were trying to do** and what happened instead.
2. **The exact error message** – a screenshot is ideal.
3. **Where in the portal** you were (page/module and the address in the browser).
4. **Which organization or tenant** you were in.
5. **The approximate time** the error occurred.

::note
Screenshot: the support page in the portal with contact details (email and phone).
::

## Before you get in touch

Many problems can be solved right away:

- Read [Errors and troubleshooting](/en/support/errors-and-troubleshooting).
- Check [Service status](/en/support/service-status) – the error may be caused by a known disruption or planned maintenance.
- Reload the page and try again.
