# Email Security

The email security module helps you publish and monitor standards that protect your email domain.

## What you can do

- **MTA-STS** — publish a policy that requires encrypted email delivery to your domain.
- **BIMI** — display your logo in recipients' inboxes (requires correctly configured DMARC).
- **Lookup tool** — check which records are published for a domain.

::warning
The portal publishes the DNS records for you, but receiving mail servers decide for themselves whether to honor them. "Published" means the record exists in DNS — not that every recipient respects it.
::
