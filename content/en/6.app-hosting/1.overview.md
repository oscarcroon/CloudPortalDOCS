# App Hosting

App Hosting is CoreIT Cloud's feature for importing, building and publishing apps from GitHub — for example apps created in Lovable.

## What you can do

- **Connect GitHub** — connect your GitHub account or an organization.
- **Import app** — bring in an app from a repository.
- **Build & publish** — build the app and publish it.
- **Secrets & environments** — manage environment variables and secrets.
- **Domains & publishing** — connect domains to your published app.
