# Overview

CloudPortal is a portal where you manage your cloud resources in one place — domains and DNS, email security, SSL/TLS certificates and your applications. This documentation is a user manual that walks through the portal's pages step by step.

## Who this documentation is for

- **Customers and end users** who use the portal for their own organization.
- **MSP partners** who manage several customer organizations on behalf of their customers (see the [Partner](/en/partner/overview) section).

## How the portal is organized

The portal is **multi-organization**: your account can have access to one or more organizations. When you sign in you always work within one selected organization, and everything you see — domains, certificates, members — belongs to that organization.

Features are split into modules. Which modules you see depends on what your organization has enabled and what your role allows.
