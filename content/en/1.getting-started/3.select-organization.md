# Select organization

Your account can have access to several organizations. You always work in **one** organization at a time, and the selected organization determines which domains, certificates, members and settings you see.

## Selecting an organization at sign-in

If you have access to more than one organization, you choose which one to work in after signing in. If you only have one organization, it's selected automatically.

## Switching organization

You can switch the active organization at any time using the organization switcher in the portal. When you switch, all content updates to the new organization.

::warning
Double-check which organization you're working in before making changes, especially if you manage several customers. Changes always apply to the active organization.
::

## No organization?

If you don't have access to any organization, you may need to complete an organization setup or wait for an administrator to grant you access. If you've received an invitation, follow the link in the invitation email to accept it.

## Next steps

Continue to the [dashboard](/en/getting-started/dashboard) to see your organization's overview.
