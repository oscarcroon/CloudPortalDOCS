# Dashboard

The dashboard is the home page after you sign in. From here you reach all of the portal's modules for the organization you're working in.

## Navigation

- **Top menu** — switch between the portal's main areas.
- **Module cards** — each enabled module (domains, certificates, app hosting and more) appears as a card you can open.
- **Organization switcher** — change which organization you're working in.
- **Profile** — reach your account settings.

## What you see

Which modules and actions appear is controlled by two things:

1. **What the organization has enabled** — administrators can turn modules on and off.
2. **Your role** — your permissions determine what you can see and do.

::note
Missing a feature you expect? It may be disabled for the organization or outside your role. Contact your administrator.
::
