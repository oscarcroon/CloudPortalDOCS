# Sign in

## Sign in with email and password

1. Go to the portal's sign-in page.
2. Enter your email address and password.
3. Confirm the security check if prompted.
4. Select **Sign in**.

## Sign in via SSO (single sign-on)

If your organization has enabled single sign-on, you sign in with your usual organization account, for example Microsoft Entra ID. Choose the option for your organization's sign-in and you'll be sent to your identity provider and back to the portal.

::note
Which sign-in method is shown depends on how your organization is configured. If the SSO option is missing but you expect it, contact your administrator.
::

## Forgot password

Select **Forgot password** on the sign-in page and enter your email address. You'll receive an email with a link to reset your password.

## Waiting for access

If your account is created but not yet assigned to an organization, you may land on a page indicating that access is pending. An administrator then needs to grant you access before you can continue.

## Next steps

Once signed in, choose which [organization](/en/getting-started/select-organization) you want to work in.
