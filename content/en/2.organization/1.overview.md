# Organization & Teams

Under organization settings you manage who has access to your organization and what they can do.

## What you can do

- **Members** — invite people, remove access and see who has access.
- **Roles and permissions** — control what different members can see and do.
- **Audit logs** — track which changes were made and by whom.
- **API tokens** — create tokens for integrations against the portal's API.
